lost&found&lostagain

lost&found&lostagain, my first full-length album, released on May 3, 2025.

It's an indie rock / emo album with some pop-punk sensibilities. I wrote these songs between 2016 and 2023, and recorded them between 2023 and 2025. I did most of the engineering and mixed the album myself.

The album is available for streaming here:

  • Bandcamp
  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • YouTube Music
  • ...and all other major streaming platforms.

I also have CDs available on a $5-15 sliding scale; see Bandcamp for photos and to order.

I was interviewed by Tom Hilton about the album for Aldora Britain Records.

You can read the interview here, the interview starts on page 11.

Someone Left The Light On

Someone Left The Light On is an EP I recorded with my band in college, Monsters in the Attic.

It's instrumental post-rock.

You can stream and download the EP for free on Bandcamp.

I played the guitar and bass, and mixed the EP. Full credits can be found on Bandcamp.

"Hum"

"Hum" is an audio essay written and performed by Julie Piñero about her and her mother's experience being simultaneously diagnosed with Tourette Syndrome. The piece was featured on the episode "Worries" of the podcast Love Me, hosted by Lu Olkowski and presented by the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation (CBC).

I engineered Julie's voiceover narration in my home studio.

You can listen to the piece on the CBC's website, or on all major streaming services. "Hum" starts 11m30s into the episode.
