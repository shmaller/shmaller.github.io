About Nick

I am a writer, photographer, and musician from Albany, NY. My work has been published in Maudlin House, Bkylner, the Adirondack Daily Enterprise, the Rensselaer Polytechnic, and Statler & Waldorf. My writing was also featured in Popular Science in a broader discussion of publicly-owned power utilities.

I graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a B.S. in Physics in May, 2016. I currently reside in Brooklyn, NY.

You can reach me via email at nick@nicholasboni.com. I'm on Instagram and LinkedIn too.
