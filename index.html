Chapter One

We were halfway through the desert when the drugs took hold. There was some kid with us, and he was freaked out to fuck. I gripped the wheel tightly as the bats started to swoop. The endless desert shimmered in the sun all around us, and my attorney's face took on the snout of a horrific anteater. I knew then I should bash his brains out, but my eyes were transfixed on the road receding ever into the distance -- nothing but monsters from here to there.
