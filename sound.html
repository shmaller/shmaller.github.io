lost&found&lostagain

lost&found&lostagain, my first full-length album, released on May 3, 2025.

It's an indie rock / emo album with some pop-punk sensibilities. I wrote these songs between 2016 and 2023, and recorded them between 2023 and 2025. I did most of the engineering and mixed the album myself.

The album is available for streaming here:

  • Bandcamp
  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • YouTube Music
  • ...and all other major streaming platforms.

I also have CDs available on a $5-15 sliding scale; see Bandcamp for photos and to order.

I was interviewed by Tom Hilton about the album for Aldora Britain Records.

You can read the interview here, the interview starts on page 11.

Sound engineering

"Hum"

"Hum" is an audio essay written by Julie Piñero and edited by Cristal Duhaime.

I engineered Julie's voiceover narration in my home studio.

The piece will debut late 2025 with the Canadian Broadcast Corporation (CBC).

"Work"

"Work" is a single by Isabelle "Bee" Rashkin and Chase Gilbertson.

I mastered the song.

You can listen to "Work" on YouTube.

"People In My House"

"People In My House" is a single by Isabelle "Bee" Rashkin.

I engineered the session in my home studio, played the bass, programmed the drums, and mixed the track.

You can listen to "People in My House" on YouTube.
